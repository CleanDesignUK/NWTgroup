About Jesse

The Story Behind North Wales Tutoring Group

Jesse founded North Wales Tutoring Group after seeing how much of a difference personalised teaching can make to a student’s confidence, understanding, and long-term progress.

With a strong academic background and extensive tutoring experience, he built North Wales Tutoring Group around the idea that lessons should feel clear, encouraging, and tailored to the individual rather than one-size-fits-all.

His goal has always been to create tutoring that feels high quality, supportive, and more accessible for families, while helping students improve both confidence and outcomes over time.

Our values

What we stand for

We want tutoring to feel high quality, personalised, encouraging, and more accessible for students and families.

Our vision

To make strong tutoring support more accessible to students across North Wales, Wales and the UK, while keeping lessons personal, encouraging and genuinely helpful.

Our mission

To help students improve understanding, build confidence and make meaningful progress through tutoring that is tailored to their level, goals and learning style.

Personalised lessons

We believe students learn best when lessons are adapted to the individual rather than delivered in a one-size-fits-all way.

Accessible support

We believe more students should be able to access quality tutoring, with options that are more realistic for families from different financial backgrounds.
