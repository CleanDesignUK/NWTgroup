Frequently asked questions

Online tutoring FAQs

Find answers about online tutoring across Wales and the UK, the subjects and levels we cover, tutor applications, franchise opportunities, lesson formats, and how to contact North Wales Tutoring Group.

Yes. North Wales Tutoring Group provides online tutoring across North Wales, wider Wales, England, and the UK. We support learners through flexible one-to-one and group online lessons, making it easier for families and adult learners to access high-quality tutoring wherever they are based. If you would like to enquire about tutoring, you can contact our team here, email us at enquiries@nwttutoring.com, or message us on WhatsApp.

We offer online tutoring for primary, 11+, SATs, KS3, GCSE, A-level, and adult learners. Our aim is to provide accessible tutoring support for different ages, academic stages, and learning goals. Whether a student needs help building confidence, improving grades, preparing for exams, or strengthening subject knowledge, we work to match them with the right tutor and lesson format. To discuss the right level of support, please visit our contact page.

We currently offer tutoring in Maths, Science, English Language, English Literature, Humanities, Economics, Politics, French, Spanish, and Health & Social Care. Subject availability can vary depending on tutor specialism and timetable, so if you are looking for a particular subject, exam board, or level, the best next step is to send us an enquiry. We will then recommend the most suitable tutoring support for your needs.

Yes. We offer one-to-one online tutoring as well as group tutoring. One-to-one tutoring is ideal for personalised support, focused exam preparation, and confidence building. Group tutoring can work well for siblings, friends, or learners who want to study together with a shared tutor. If you would like to discuss the best lesson format, please get in touch here and we will help guide you.

You can enquire about tutoring by using our contact page, emailing enquiries@nwttutoring.com, or sending us a message on WhatsApp. To help us match you with the right tutor, it is useful to include the student’s level, subject, preferred day and time, and any goals or topics they would like to cover.

Yes. We are growing our tutoring network and welcome enquiries from people interested in building a tutoring franchise in their local area. Our franchise model is designed for those who want to grow a local tutoring brand with website support, online infrastructure, and access to a wider tutor network. If you would like to know more about joining the franchise, please visit our contact page, email enquiries@nwttutoring.com, or message us on WhatsApp.

If you are interested in joining our tutor network, you can apply by getting in touch through our contact page, emailing enquiries@nwttutoring.com, or sending us a message on WhatsApp. When applying, it helps to include the subjects and levels you teach, your experience, your location, and your typical availability.

North Wales Tutoring Group is built around accessible online tutoring, personalised support, and flexible options for different learners. We work with tutors from a range of academic and professional backgrounds, helping families and learners find suitable support across different subjects, levels, and budgets. Our goal is to make the process simple, supportive, and effective for students across Wales and the wider UK. To start your enquiry, please contact us here.
