What matters in your application

Show us what makes you a strong tutor

Your core details

Full name, email address, phone number, and UK location.

Your experience

Tell us about the teaching, tutoring, academic, or professional background that helps you support students well.

Subjects you tutor

Be clear about the subjects where you are strongest and most confident, rather than listing everything too broadly.

Levels you support

Let us know which learners you are best suited to help, from Primary and KS3 through to GCSE, A-Level, 11+, or adult learning.

DBS status

We want to understand your current DBS position clearly, whether checked, not yet checked, or in progress.

Your introduction

Your introduction matters. This is your chance to show your personality, communication style, and why students would benefit from learning with you.
