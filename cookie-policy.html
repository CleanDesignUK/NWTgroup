Cookie Policy

How cookies are used on our website

This Cookie Policy explains what cookies are, how North Wales Tutoring Group uses them, and how you can manage your cookie preferences on our website.

1. What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help websites function properly, remember preferences, and collect information about how people use the site.

2. Why we use cookies

We may use cookies to:

  • Make the website work properly
  • Remember basic preferences
  • Understand how visitors use the website
  • Improve functionality, content, and performance
  • Support security and prevent misuse

3. Types of cookies we may use

  • Essential cookies: Required for core website functionality
  • Performance and analytics cookies: Help us understand website usage and performance
  • Preference cookies: Help remember settings or choices

4. Consent

Where required, we ask for your consent before using non-essential cookies. You can accept or decline through our cookie banner.

5. Managing cookies

You can manage cookies through your browser settings. You can also use the cookie banner on our website where available. Disabling some cookies may affect website functionality.

6. Third-party services

We may use trusted third-party services for analytics, website tools, form handling, or embedded content. These services may place their own cookies depending on how they operate.

7. Updates to this Cookie Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page.

8. Contact us

If you have questions about our use of cookies, please contact us through our website or email enquiries@nwttutoring.com.
